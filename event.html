My Baby's Favourite Food - Event

We are hosting an event for all moms, out there. Anything particular your baby enjoyed eating? Please share it with us!

Here are the guidelines for participating :

  1. Write a blog post giving us the recipe of a dish your child liked as a baby. Preferably, the post should have a picture of the dish.
  2. Link your blog post back to this event announcement http://babyfoodrecipes.in/event.
  3. Submit your entry using the below form

You can submit old blog posts too if you like. 3 lucky people will be gifted my e-book, 60 Great Baby Food Recipes. This book is currently being sold on Amazon. You can also send me a picture of the baby who liked this baby food recipe you are sending. I will include him in the hall of fame on this site :-)

So get creative and send in your entries on or before June 15th, 2012.
