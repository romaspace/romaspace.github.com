About the Book

8 months to 2 years is one of the very critical phase in a child's growth. 8 months is around the time when the child starts having food. Child's tastebuds are not fully developed and neither is her digestive system. There are a lot of do's and don't around this age which your child's pediatric would have instructed you about.

The book is divided in three phases coinciding with child's growth. The first part focuses when the child is 6-8 months old. These recipes introduces food to the baby which gives her subtle taste of what is to come while ensuring that the item is high in nutrition.

When my first daughter was 6 months old, I started researching on various solid foods that I could give her.

Roma Sharma - The Author

With just plain sunlight in her terrace and her assistant (read husband) holding the scrimp, she has some of the most apealing photos of her concoction.

One year since she started blogging and while raising Raina who was around a year old she created 100 recipes at an average of about 2 a week. Not only were these recipes her own creation, she completed it with a fine photo shoot.
